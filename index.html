what we offer

blood donation

Blood donation is a lifesaving care, essential for patients' survival from surgery, cancer treatments, traumatic injuries and chronic illnesses. The need for blood is constant, with a global yearly turnout of age-eligible people averaging about 3% only.

grief counselling

Our grief and bereavement counselling program is designed to cater individuals struggling with the loss of a loved one. Our counselors are well equipped to help you find suitable ways for coping with your grief and loss.

assisted living

Our main goal is to support you or your loved one in maintaining a high quality of life, ensuring a comfortable, satisfying living experience, while maximizing on individual independence, stability and peace of mind.

organ donation

Organ donation is a life-giving and life-enhancing opportunity, with the potential of saving multiple lives. AfterLyf seeks to provide a safe and efficient conduit between donors and recipients potentially improving the quality of life for many people.

who we are

AfterLyf is a dedicated multi-dimensional healthcare service organization, focusing on blood and organ donation, grief and bereavement counselling, and assisted living. It is our core belief that humans have a right to a dignified way of living, and can further enhance their purpose post-mortem as organ donors. Our main emphasis is on the freedom of choice. Our greatest belief is in the innate desire and ability to come together as a society and help each other out. Our foundation is on that core value of togetherness, love, care, peace and stability, and AfterLyf is determined to partner with you in this journey and beyond.

afterlyf healthcare solutions

haematology

We conduct specialized hematological tests of the blood and blood components for early diagnosis, treatment and management of a wide range of benign and malignant disorders.

lab services

We have professional laboratory diagnostic services for our in-patients and out-patients.

emergency services

Our call emergency center is active 24/7 to ensure public health and safety through prompt response to different emergencies.
